Manchester City Official Store

Welcome to the Manchester City Store, your official store for the team's merchandise.
The official Manchester City Store is operated by stichd, a member of the PUMA group and an internationally recognised, licensed merchandise
partner. Being a PUMA company, our close relationship with sports enables us to be the first to deliver you the very latest official licensed
products, including special editions and exclusive products not available anywhere else.

stichd

At stichd sportsmerchandising, we design, develop, and distribute licensed sports merchandise worldwide. When ordering from the official Man
City store, you’re ordering directly from the club. As a fan, this gives you the guarantee you’re getting 100% official, licensed merchandise every
time.

What our customers say about us

We’re always striving to be the fastest, simplest and easiest way to get all your favourite team apparel and merchandise. As fellow fans, we share
your passion for football and always welcome your feedback on how we could make your shopping experience even better. We’re very proud of
our customer feedback, and our friendly customer support team are always keen to help should you need it.
